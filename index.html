    Our Mission and Goals

    Gender / Protection

    focuses on prevention of Violence against women, children and the aged through setting up relevant community-based structures,

    Education

    Our child education program focuses on two aspects:

    (a) Children with Hearing and Speech Impairment

    (b) Early Childhood Development.

    Health

    Our Health Program aims at delivering sustainable Sexual Reproductive Health and Rights.

    Improves MNCH, Malaria control and HIV/AIDS.

    Orphans and Vulnerable Children

    PCI supports Government of Uganda’s effort to ensure that children’s early learning at all different stages of development is implemented and supported

    Food Security.

    The economy of Uganda is built on the agricultural sector and yet 70% of Ugandans are employed by subsistence agriculture with an estimated 23.7% of the GDP is from Agriculture.

    Climate Change & Environmental Protection.

    Our approach aims at providing sustainable and community inclusive interventions that aim at addressing the effects of climate change and environmental degradation
